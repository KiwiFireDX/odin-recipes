Omurice

Home

Omurice is the ultimate savory seduction—fluffy, buttery eggs barely set and draped like silk over a mound of tender, ketchup-infused fried rice, rich with bits of chicken, onion, and the gentle sweetness of sautéed vegetables. The omelet is so delicate it quivers at the touch, its golden surface splitting open to reveal a molten, custard-like center that oozes slowly over the rice in a slow, sensual spill. A drizzle of tangy-sweet demi-glace or ketchup glistens on top, adding a bold, glossy finish that pulls the whole dish together in one decadent, comforting bite. It's warm, silky, and deeply satisfying—like comfort food dressed for a romantic evening.

Ingredients

    For The Chicken Rice

  • 1/4 onion
  • 3 oz boneless, chicken thigh
  • 1 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • ground black pepper
  • Japanese short-grained rice
  • 5 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

    • For the Omellette

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter

Directions

    To Make Chicken Rice

  1. Prepare cooked rice
  2. Chop Onions
  3. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces
  4. Put onions in the pan to cook then add chicken
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Cook until chicken is no longer pink
  7. Reduce heat then add ketchup and worcestershire sauce
  8. Add your cooked rice to the pan
  9. Season with remaining salt and pepper

    10. To Make the Omurice

  10. Beat 2 eggs into a bowl
  11. Add salt and pepper
  12. heat butter in a pan
  13. Add the beated egg when pan is hot
  14. Move the eggs around in the pan to create soft, small curds
  15. When egg is half cooked add the chicken rice to the center of the egg
  16. Fold the egg around the rice
  17. Put onto a plate and its ready to serve!
